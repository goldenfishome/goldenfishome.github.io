About Me

Exploring the data science world

Welcome to my website. My name is Yujia Jin, you can call me Sian. I am a graduate student of Georgetown Data Science and Analytics Program with a background in Master of Pharmacy. Though from pharmacy to data science is a big jump, I am always keen to find the connects between these two fields and look for data analytics tool to facilitate the development of the healthcare field. My unique background brings up more diversity into the fields and definitely adds up a new perspective of thinking into data science problems.

I am very passionate about data analyzing, and enjoy the process of finding pattern and drawing conclusions from seemingly unordered data. I am proficient in a number of languages including Python, R, SQL, JavaScript, Microsoft Office suite, and Tableau, and have interests in NLP, Machine learning, Big Data, Data visualization and etc. Check my links below to see some of projects I did.
